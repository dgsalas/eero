Our Services

This is a brief description of the services we provide

Interior Design & Planning

Whether you want to enact your plan now or later, we can prepare design concepts, color and fabric schemes, drawings, and layouts for entire spaces or specific rooms. We also offer space planning, paint selection, and custom furniture/cabinetry design.

Interior Architecture

In addition to furnishings and décor, Isolina Mallon Interiors can transform any interior space. Our range spans bath, kitchen, and full home remodels to office, restaurant, hotel, and other commercial jobs.

Purchasing & Installation

Let us take your newly-conceived design and implement the entire project for you, saving you time and effort. We place orders and track progress to ensure all items are made to your specifications, stored as necessary, and installed properly onsite.

Construction Management

Isolina Mallon Interiors partners with contractors, architects, and subcontractors to ensure high quality and timely results. We manage the inevitable “unforeseeable circumstance” so you don’t have to, and streamline communications to lessen your burden.

Custom Cabinetry & Furniture Design

From built-in cabinets to architectural details, furniture, artwork, and lighting – if you can dream it, we can build it. Isolina Mallon Interiors works with skilled artisans and contractors to create unique and tailored interior environments.

Consultation

Have questions or need a second opinion? Don’t know where to start? We offer expertise, advice, suggestions, and feedback on everything from paint colors to resale strategies to construction plans. Onsite visits are a great way to test out ideas and avoid costly mistakes.
